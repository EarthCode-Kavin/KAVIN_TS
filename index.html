Thesis

Thamirabarani River Basin

Geomorphic and Tectonic Influences on Longitudinal and Cross Profiles

Investigated geomorphic and tectonic controls on river profile development using DEM analysis, longitudinal and cross-profile extraction, morphometric indices, and tectonic interpretation of knickpoints and gradient anomalies.
